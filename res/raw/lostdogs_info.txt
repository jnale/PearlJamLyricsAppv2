Lost Dogs Rarities/B-sides Double Album - This 2-disc album features 30 tracks compiled from Pearl Jam's extensive b-side archives and from various benefit albums and soundtracks. None of the included tracks have been previously featured on any of the band's studio albums.


Release date: November 11, 2003

Cover Concept by Jeff Ament
Cover/Back Photos by Jeff Ament
Additional Polaroid Photography by Ed Vedder
Layout and Design by Brad Klausen

Liner notes corrections:
Hold On: from the Ten sessions
Yellow Ledbetter: drums - Dave Krusen
Produced by - Rick Parishar
